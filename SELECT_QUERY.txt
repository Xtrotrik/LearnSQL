*SELECT [QUERY]*

1>TO VIEW WHOLE TABLE 
-SELECT * FROM [TABLE_NAME]

2>TO VIEW SELECTED FIELD
-SELECT [FIELD_NAME],[FIELD_NAME] FROM [TABLE_NAME]

3>TO VIEW SELECTED FIELD AS CUSTOM NAME
-SELECT [FIELD_NAME] AS [CUSTOM_NAME],
[FIELD_NAME] AS [CUSTOM_NAME]
FROM [TABLE_NAME]

4>TO VIEW SELECTED FIELD WITH CONDITION
-SELECT * FROM [TABLE_NAME]
WHERE [FIELD_NAME] [CONDITION]
